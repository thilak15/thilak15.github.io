Our Mission

GirlScript is working to change lives by imparting tech education and relevant skills.

Awareness about Tech

Help us reach tier-two and tier-three cities where people do not know how tech education can change their lives.

scholarship and fellowship

Getting recognized is an important part of anyone's career. With GirlScript, know exactly where you fit in.

young and enthusiastic mentor

Finding like minded people who resonate with your goal is extremely important. Find such enthusiastic people through GirlScript's network.

Diversity

Know how Diversity & Inclusion can play an important role in generating ideas, team building and getting the maximum output.

women in tech

GirlScript is NOT a women only organsation but it has equal opportunities for everyone and thus 50% reservation for women in tech.

internship opportunities

Looking for a perfect team to work with? GirlScript's diverse and strong network can help you.

remote jobs

Nobody likes to sit in a cubicle and remote jobs are hottest trend. We are now aiming to match you with your perfect work from home job.

skills over money

95% of our programs do not require any money. We are looking out for passionate individuals whom we can transform.

Our Programs

Education Outreach Program

5000+ Students Impacted
After the lockdown was announced, we gave free scholarships to 5000+ students and taught them 30+ courses including Technical & Non - Technical Topics.

Know More

GirlScript Summer Of Code

3,500+ Participants
GirlScript Summer of Code is the 3 month long Open Source program during summers conducted by GirlScript Foundation, started in 2018, with an aim to help beginners get started with Open Source Development.
Know More

LetsPy

3,000+ Students Impacted
LetsPy is a two day bootcamp for the people who are new to the language or want to brush up their skills. Especially for the people from tier- two and tier- three cities in India who do not have enough resources.
Know More

GirlScript India Summit

2,500+ Students Impacted
GirlScript India Summit is our Annual and biggest celebration of technology, talent, entrepreneurship and diversity. It's a Two Days Event including Hackathon, Tech-Talks, Workshops.
Know More

Uplift

This new global project aims at helping everyone looking for opportunities to leverage their knowledge, skills, and potential to higher levels. If you're a student wanting to learn tech stacks like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Angular, React, Node Js, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and many more, if you are the one with enough knowledge of skills mentioned above then this program is for all of you!
